Harry Lane
Use Case : Acquire Land

Primary Actor: Player

Stakeholders and Interests:
- Player: Wants to gain additional land for development

Preconditions:
- Previous auctioning has ended, or game has started from the beginning

Success Guarantee:
- Player gains additional land. Moves to another player, if there is one
  to acquire land or if not, moves on to develop land for the first player.

Main Success Scenario:
- 1) Player selects a plot of land
- 2) Land is added to players inventory

Extensions:
- 1) A random plot of land is auctioned off for all players to try
     and buy. If bought, a player would acquire two plots of land
     in one turn.

Frequency of Occurance: Happens once per player per turn
